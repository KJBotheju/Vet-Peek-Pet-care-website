Our Services

At PetLand, we believe our customers view their animal friends as extensions of their family. This is why our package of services, which includes wellness programmers as well as disease diagnostics and treatment, is designed to help you connect, care for, and cure your pets.

We offer the following services for domestic and exotic animals. These include wellness programs as well as disease diagnosis and treatment, including surgery, dentistry and diagnostic imaging (XRay/Scan).

Comprehensive Pain Control

The signs of pain in animals are very subtle, requiring trained eyes and hands to recognize and treat it

Advanced Anesthesia

PetLand uses gas anesthesia (known as inhalant anesthesia) for all surgical procedures. Inhalant anesthesia is safer for many reasons

Pet Salon

Our specially trained teams areavailable to keep your pet wellgroomed and looking fine at all times.

24 Hour Emergency Service

PetLand was among the first veterinary practices to offer 24-hour emergency services, initiated 10 years ago.

House Visits

If you need us on a busy day,our teams are happy to help you out by bringing our services to your doorstep

Laboratory Services

PetLand’s state-of-the-art laboratory is operated by staff who bring over 10 years’ experience with animals

Dental Services

Regular and proactive dental hygiene and care of animal companions can make a massive difference in ensuring strong teeth.
